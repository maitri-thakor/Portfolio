About Me

I'm Maitri Thakor, a passionate Full-Stack Developer based in Wellington, New Zealand. I recently graduated with an NZQA Level 6 Certificate in Applied Software Development from Dev Academy Aotearoa, where I completed an intensive 15-week program that honed my skills in both front-end and back-end technologies.

My technical expertise includes HTML, CSS, JavaScript, ASP.NET, C#, and frameworks such as React.js and Node.js. I am proficient in SQL, including SQLite and SQL Server. I am skilled in UI/UX design using tools like DaisyUI, Tailwind CSS, Canva, and Figma. I truly enjoy finding solutions and consider myself a problem-solving person who thrives in a team environment.

I am committed to continuous learning and am currently preparing for the AWS Cloud Practitioner certification. I have been working on various AWS services like S3, EC2, RDS, IAM, QuickSight, and Lex to enhance my understanding. Additionally, I am learning Python and developing my full-stack applications to address real-world problems.

Outside of coding, I enjoy exploring the outdoors, engaging in photography, and practicing meditation, which helps me maintain focus and clarity in my work. I am always open to new challenges and opportunities to collaborate on impactful projects.
